Palayan Road Hazard Map

Navigational Tips

Welcome to the Palayan Road Hazard Mapping System. Before you explore the map, here are some important tips to help you navigate and understand the hazard information:

Understanding Hazard Markers

Different colored markers represent different types of hazards. Red indicates fire hazards, blue shows flood risks, and orange marks structural issues.

Search Functionality

Use the search bar to find specific locations or hazards. You can search by address, hazard type, or keyword.

Filtering Hazards

Use the category filters in the sidebar to show only specific types of hazards. This helps you focus on the risks most relevant to you.

Street View Details

Click on any hazard marker and then select "View Street Details" to see images and more information about specific locations.

Reporting Hazards

If you discover a new hazard, use the report button to notify authorities and help keep your community safe.

Stay safe and be aware of your surroundings when navigating hazardous areas.
